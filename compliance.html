What We Handle

Everything Your Back Office Needs

DOT compliance is not optional — violations mean fines, out-of-service orders, and damaged reputation. Most owner-operators and small fleets don't have a dedicated compliance officer. Haulxify provides that function at a fraction of the cost.

Book a free consultation

ELD Log Monitoring

Daily review of ELD logs for HOS violations, missing entries, and data inconsistencies — flagged and corrected proactively.

Driver Qualification Files

DQ files maintained for every driver — MVR, medical certificates, drug testing records, and employment history — complete and current.

DOT Drug & Alcohol Program

Random testing pool management, pre-employment testing coordination, and clearinghouse reporting handled correctly.

Carrier Authority Maintenance

MC/DOT authority status monitored, insurance filings maintained with FMCSA, and BOC-3 agent kept current.

Permit Management

Oversize/overweight permits, trip permits, fuel permits, and state authority filings obtained and tracked.

CSA Score Monitoring

Your CSA scores tracked monthly with alerts on violations that affect your safety rating and shipper relationships.

Pre-Audit Preparation

Mock audit walk-throughs, file organization, and gap identification before FMCSA or state audits.

Compliance Calendar & Alerts

Renewal dates, expiration alerts, and filing deadlines tracked and flagged in advance — nothing expires unnoticed.
