Questions About Pricing?

You're paying for a dedicated agent's working hours — not per action or per load. If your agent works 8 hours handling dispatch, calls, and paperwork, you pay for 8 hours. Simple as that. Monthly plans bundle those hours at a discounted rate.
No setup fee — ever. The minimum on hourly plans is 40 hours/month (roughly 2 hours per business day). Monthly plans lock in a block of hours. There are no long-term contracts — you can cancel or adjust at the end of any billing cycle.
